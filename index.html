Welcome

Welcome to this space dedicated to the MAIDD Reading Group co-organized by Valence Discovery and Mila - Quebec AI Institute. Our aim is to offer a variety of talks and a discussion platform to this diverse community: from open-source tools to advance in traditional computational techniques to latest ML frameworks and applications.
A wide range of drug discovery related topics will also be covered in these talks to reflect the vibrant diversity of tools and methodologies in the field and community:

  • Applications of machine learning in computational molecular design
  • Representation learning for small molecules and macromolecules
  • Prediction of molecular properties and bioactivities
  • Machine learning for quantum chemistry and molecular dynamics
  • Generative models for de novo molecular design
  • Active learning & uncertainty quantification
  • Multiparameter optimization and compound selection
  • Interpretable and explainable activity models
  • Reaction prediction, retrosynthesis, and synthesis planning
